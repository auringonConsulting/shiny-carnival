why auringon?

The word Auringon — a derivation of the Finnish word for “sun” — is closely tied to our firm’s identity. Specifically, we’re inspired by the sun’s unique role in the Finnish landscape throughout the seasons.

In winter, the sun provides the energy necessary for the auroras to embellish the dark sky with color. In summer, the midnight radiance shrouds the darkness of previous seasons.

Auringon Consulting strives to emulate the versatility, reliability and influence of the auringon. Our boutique consulting firm is dedicated to providing our clients with bespoke solutions for their unique needs.
