Approach & Offerings

Auringon is the consulting practice of Jonathan Internicola. We stand up the operation a plan needs, and step out once your team runs it. We work with startup founders, governments and non-profits, and prime contractors. Here's how an engagement works, and the shapes it takes.

Approach

How an engagement works

Each engagement starts with what you want, and what you're working against. From there I lay out a few ways in: what each involves, what you walk away with. We pick one together.

No two are the same, but I scope them the same way: a read or a build, me delivering or your team, a thing built or a number moved.

Offerings

The shapes an engagement takes

Direct

Engagements I take on myself.

Read

You're not seeing the return from a change. I find exactly why it isn't producing, and what to do next.

Build

There's a gap. A capability, a feature, a team, a program. I help your team stand it up, then step out once they can run it without me.

Number

A number has to move for the people you answer to. I work across your teams, processes, and systems to turn it around.

Activation

You're lean and running fine, but something you already own could earn more. I work out how to turn it into revenue without raising costs elsewhere.
